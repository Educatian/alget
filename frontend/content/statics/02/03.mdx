## From Particles to Rigid Bodies

For a **particle**, we only needed force equilibrium:

$$
\sum \vec{F} = 0
$$

For a **rigid body**, we also need moment equilibrium:

$$
\sum \vec{M}_O = 0
$$

Together, these give us **6 equations in 3D** (3 force, 3 moment) or **3 equations in 2D**:

$$
\boxed{
\begin{aligned}
\sum F_x &= 0 \\
\sum F_y &= 0 \\
\sum M_O &= 0
\end{aligned}
}
$$
